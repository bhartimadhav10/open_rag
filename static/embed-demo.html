OpenRAG embed demo

The chat bubble in the bottom-right corner is the OpenRAG widget. Click it.

Add it to your own site

<script src="/embed.js"
        data-openrag-url=""
        data-openrag-title="Ask our docs"
        data-openrag-color="#22d3ee"
        data-openrag-position="bottom-right"
        async></script>

For this to work from a different origin, set CORS_ORIGINS in your OpenRAG server's .env to include your site (e.g. CORS_ORIGINS=https://your-site.com).
